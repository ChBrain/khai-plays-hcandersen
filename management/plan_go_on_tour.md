# Plan: Go on Tour

## Taxonomy

A **mandate**: the house Roadie's outbound checklist, run when this house's work
is sent to a Venue.

## Owner

- Owner: [The Roadie](position_roadie.md)

## Direction

This house's work reaches its audience at a Venue, in the shape that Venue can
take. Produced once, experienced wherever it is toured.

## Orders

The Roadie composes and stages, never authors. What to ship and where (the Venue,
the collections, the engines) is the Roadie's judgment; the deterministic
packages compute the invariant.

## Implementation

The tour toolchain; the Venue profile (kind and source); the deployment composed
as Standard plus Adaption with engines injected at Knowledge.

## Targets

- [ ] the Venue deployment is composed: Standard plus Adaption, engines at Knowledge
- [ ] the Venue's constraints are met (for example a file or size limit)
- [ ] the artifact is built: an interactive bundle, or a rendered publication
- [ ] the deployment is delivered to the Venue
