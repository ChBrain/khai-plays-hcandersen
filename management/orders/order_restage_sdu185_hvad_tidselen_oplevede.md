# Order: Restage SDU 185 Hvad Tidselen oplevede

## Direction

Campaign 2 of the content-quality treatment charter: the review found the
former board feeding the thistle to the donkey (which in the tale is
tethered and cannot reach it) and blurring the pivotal Scottish lady into a
generic compassionate girl. The production is restaged from the actual
"Hvad Tidselen oplevede" (1869): the thistle outside the manor fence, the
Scottish young lady who picks its bloom as Scotland's emblem and seals the
engagement, and the tale's own final beat. The board is the smallest of the
campaign so far because the source is a single-reversal object fable: one
persona, one position, one piece, one place, one pitch, three plots.
