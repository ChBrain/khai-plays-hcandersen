# Order: Restage SDU 037 Tolvte Aften

rich man in a chandeliered hall; the sourcing campaign verified the actual
Twelfth Evening of Billedbog uden Billeder (Hersholt numbering, read at the
evening's own page): Pompeii, the Street of the Tombs, German soldiers at
dice, strangers led through the dead city's lava-paved streets, the Temple
of Venus with the weeping willow between its columns, Vesuvius black as
coal, and the great lady singer who in jest mounts the amphitheater's
ancient stage and sings - then, three minutes later, the emptied scene, the
applause forgotten while the ruin stands for centuries. The production is
restaged from the verified text, and by this order the board's provenance
class moves from free to sourced, declared per file.
