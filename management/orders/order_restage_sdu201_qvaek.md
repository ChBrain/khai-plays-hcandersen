# Order: Restage SDU 201 Qvaek

## Direction

Campaign 2 of the content-quality treatment charter: the review and the
source verification found the former board staging a boy stoning a toad,
an image belonging to a different tale. The production is restaged from
the actual posthumous satire "Qvaek" (first printed 1926): the forest
animals found a critical journal, the frog edits it as the self-styled
critic Qvaekling, and the bee, ant, mole, rooster and flies parade as
would-be contributors. The board is sized to the source per the
no-template rule: a press-satire ensemble of twelve files, with the bee,
ant and mole as one collapsed persona because the source treats them as
one voice.

## Orders

1. The Playwright (Hans Christian Andersen) must author the restaged
   board: personas Qvaekling, Haandvaerkerne, Hanen, Fluerne; positions
   Kritiker, Bidragyder; the piece Qvaek (the journal); place Lysningen;
   pitch Satirisk; plots stiftelsen, bidragene; in authentic Danish.
2. The Theatre Manager (Edvard Collin) must verify fidelity to the
   posthumous satire: no boy, no stones, no ditch anywhere on the board.
3. The Roadie (Vilhelm Pedersen) must rebuild the registry through its
   single writer and ship as a `patch` changeset at the standing count.
