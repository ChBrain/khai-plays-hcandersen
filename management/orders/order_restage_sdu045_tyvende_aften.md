# Order: Restage SDU 045 Tyvende Aften

## Direction

The review flagged this board as a generic invented tableau; the research
verified the actual Twentieth Evening of Billedbog uden Billeder (Hersholt
numbering, read at the evening's own page): Rome, the ruins of an imperial
palace on one of the seven hills, the old woman and her granddaughter in
the clay house between marble columns, half of Rome and St. Peter's dome
seen through a keyhole, and the earthen pitcher shattering on the marble
steps - the child's tears over a worthless thing amid priceless ruins, the
bell-rope not dared. The production is restaged from the verified text,
and by this order the board's provenance class moves from free to sourced,
declared per file.
