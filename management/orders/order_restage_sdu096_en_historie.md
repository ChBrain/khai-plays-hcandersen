# Order: Restage SDU 096 En Historie

## Direction

Campaign 2 of the content-quality treatment charter: the review found the
former board staged an invented poet-judged-at-market allegory sharing only
its title with Andersen's tale. The production is restaged from the actual
"En Historie" (1851): the pastor who preaches eternal damnation, his gentle
wife darkened by the doctrine, her dream-visitation asking him to show one
soul mercy cannot reach, the night journey past sinners' fates in which he can
condemn none, and the tearful waking beside his living wife into a ministry of
grace. The board is sized to the source per the no-template rule: an intimate
two-persona dream journey, no plan, because mercy arrives as revelation, not
scheme.

## Orders

1. The Playwright (Hans Christian Andersen) must author the restaged board:
   two personas (Praesten, Hustruen), two positions (Doemmende, Formildende),
   three places (Kirken, Hjemmet, Natten), two processes (Formoerkelsen,
   Droemmerejsen), one piece (Ilden), one pitch (Naaden), three plots
   (praedikenen, droemmebesoeget, opvaagningen), all in authentic Danish.
2. The Theatre Manager (Edvard Collin) must verify fidelity to the 1851 tale
   and the canon conformance of every file.
3. The Roadie (Vilhelm Pedersen) must rebuild the registry through its single
   writer and ship the restaging as a `patch` changeset at the standing count.

## Implementation

One `play/` pull request carrying this order and the restaged
`plays/sdu096_en_historie/` board. The former allegory's files are removed;
every Company entry is linked from the hub and every link resolves.
