# Order: Restage the Early Evenings

## Direction

Campaign 3 of the content-quality treatment charter, now unblocked: the
complete Hersholt text of Billedbog uden Billeder was sourced evening by
evening at the register's own pages (the precondition the docket set in
`order_rule_the_docket.md`). This order restages the first sourced batch,
the sixth through the tenth evenings, each from its verified text and each
sized to that text, never to the fifteen-file template. The five boards
had staged invention: the sixth a garret student, the seventh a forest
lake, the eighth a tower prisoner, the ninth a market juggler, the tenth a
forest poet, none of it in Andersen. By this order their provenance class
moves from free to sourced, declared per file.

The verified content, per evening:

- Sixth (`sdu031`): Uppsala's plain and the turf hills of Odin, Thor, and
  Freya, where travelers cut their names into the grass; a poet on the
  hilltop empties a silver-ringed mead horn and whispers a name a cloud
  then hides.
- Seventh (`sdu032`): the coastal highway past a Viking's grave, and the
  procession of passersby who each misread nature's poetry, until a poor
  girl closes the evening.
- Eighth (`sdu033`): the moonless evening, the Moon never rising; the lone
  watcher recalls what the Moon has witnessed through history until a
  single beam breaks through as a good-night greeting.
- Ninth (`sdu034`): Greenland, the aurora and the dance, the drummer's
  seal-hunt song and the song-duel, the dying man sewn into skins who
  chooses the sea, the icebergs his tombstone.
- Tenth (`sdu035`): the old maid in her yellow satin, her unchanging life
  and her death, the runaway hearse, the fallen coffin, and the lark's
  morning hymn.

## Orders

1. The Playwright (Hans Christian Andersen) must author the five restaged
   boards from the verified evenings, each board built to its source's
   scale and genre: the moonless eighth runs shortest, the two-scene ninth
   runs longest, and each play's Stakes chapter justifies its shape.
2. The Theatre Manager (Edvard Collin) must verify each board's fidelity to
   its verified evening and confirm the free-to-sourced class move is
   declared in every file.
3. The Roadie (Vilhelm Pedersen) must rebuild the registry through its
   single writer and ship the batch as a `patch` changeset at the standing
   count.

## Implementation

One `play/` pull request carrying this order and the five restaged boards
(`sdu031`, `sdu032`, `sdu033`, `sdu034`, `sdu035`); the invented files are
removed; every link resolves; each reference warrant records the Billedbog
origin at the evening's own page.
