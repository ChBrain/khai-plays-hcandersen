# Order: Restage SDU 133 De Vises Steen

## Direction

Campaign 2 of the content-quality treatment charter: the review found the
former board staging a generic seeker-versus-shadow allegory carrying two
mutually exclusive, pre-checked plans. The production is restaged from the
actual "De Vises Steen" (1858): the wise man in the Tree of the Sun, his
four sons each mastering one sense and ruined in the world by the Devil's
tailored traps, and the blind daughter whose journey of faith ends with
"Tro" written on the book's blank page. The board is sized to the source
per the no-template rule, with a composite Soennerne persona for the four
parallel failures and the one genuinely earned plan: the Devil's schemes.

## Orders

1. The Playwright (Hans Christian Andersen) must author the restaged board:
   personas Faderen, Soennerne, Datteren, Djaevelen; positions
   Laeremesteren, Sansesoegende, Troende, Fristeren; the piece Bogen; places
   Solens Trae, Verden; processes Fristelsen, Aabenbaringen; the plan
   Raenkerne with standing, unchecked intent; pitch Tro; plots udsendelsen,
   fristelserne, hjemkomsten; in authentic Danish.
2. The Theatre Manager (Edvard Collin) must verify fidelity to the 1858
   tale, the Tro ending, and that no plan pre-resolves the story.
3. The Roadie (Vilhelm Pedersen) must rebuild the registry through its
   single writer and ship as a `patch` changeset at the standing count.

## Implementation

One `play/` pull request carrying this order and the restaged
`plays/sdu133_de_vises_steen/` board; the allegory files and the
contradictory plans are removed; every link resolves.
