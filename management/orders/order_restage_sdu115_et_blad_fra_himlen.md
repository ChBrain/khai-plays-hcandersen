# Order: Restage SDU 115 Et Blad fra Himlen

## Direction

Campaign 2 of the content-quality treatment charter: the review found the
former board staging an invented leaf-under-magnifying-glass plot with
angel, child, and scholar figures. The production is restaged from the
actual "Et Blad fra Himlen" (1853): the heavenly leaf grows into a plant no
one can classify, mocked and finally rooted up by the swineherd; only the
devout girl recognizes it truly, and its grace is buried with her, while the
inconsolable King is left exactly as he was before. The ending's irony is
kept intact: the King is never healed. The board is sized to the source per
the no-template rule: compact, two plots, one load-bearing piece, no plan.
