# Sixth Evening: Reference

## Line of Work

Staging Hans Christian Andersen's _Sjette Aften_ (SDU-031). Månen fortæller om en digter, der på Upsalas gravhøje hvisker et navn, vinden skal tie med.

## Origin

Hans Christian Andersen, _Sjette Aften_, the Sixth Evening (Hersholt numbering) of _Billedbog uden Billeder_, catalogued as SDU-031 (register vid 31) in the register of the H.C. Andersen Centret at Syddansk Universitet, the authoritative source for the Danish texts, titles, and publication history.

| Source                   | Key Work / Event                                                            | Scope                                                                    |

| **H.C. Andersen**        | _Sjette Aften_ (SDU-031)                                                    | The tale the production stages.                                          |

## Restrictions

What the production refuses to model, and to whom it delegates.

- **The register is external**: this warrant answers for one staged tale; the full Andersen catalogue and its SDU-031 numbering live in the SDU register, not here.
- **Cross-play context**: strict local isolation. Every link stays within this play's directory.
