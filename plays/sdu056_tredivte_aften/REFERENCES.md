# Thirtieth Evening: Reference

## Line of Work

Staging Hans Christian Andersen's _Tredivte Aften_ (SDU-056). Månen kigger ind i et vognskur om natten og ser rejsende sove trygt, mens en fattig musikantfamilie drømmer om deres savn.

## Origin

Hans Christian Andersen, _Tredivte Aften_, the Thirtieth Evening (Hersholt numbering) of _Billedbog uden Billeder_, catalogued as SDU-056 (register vid 56) in the register of the H.C. Andersen Centret at Syddansk Universitet, the authoritative source for the Danish texts, titles, and publication history.

## Restrictions

What the production refuses to model, and to whom it delegates.

- **The register is external**: this warrant answers for one staged tale; the full Andersen catalogue and its SDU-056 numbering live in the SDU register, not here.
- **Cross-play context**: strict local isolation. Every link stays within this play's directory.
