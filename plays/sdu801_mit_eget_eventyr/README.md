# SDU 801: Mit eget Eventyr uden Digtning

This play is a structural staging of H.C. Andersen's first autobiography, _Mit eget Eventyr uden Digtning_ (originally published in German as _Das Märchen meines Lebens ohne Dichtung_ in 1847).

## Progressive Staging Scope

Because the autobiography contains numerous references to his other fairy tales, this production is designed as a progressive work-in-progress:

1. We draw directly from the autobiography text (SDU 801).
2. We extract the stories and milestones mentioned within the book and model them as plots over time.
3. The initial version focuses on the **1844 encounters with Jacob Grimm** (in Berlin and Weimar), which are highly load-bearing for representing the system of the author's reputation, peer recognition, and international fame.
