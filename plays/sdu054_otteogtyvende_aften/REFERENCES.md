# Twenty-Eighth Evening: Reference

## Line of Work

Staging Hans Christian Andersen's _Otteogtyvende Aften_ (SDU-054). Månen ser en udmattet svane synke mod det stille hav og rejse sig alene mod solopgangen.

## Origin

Hans Christian Andersen, _Otteogtyvende Aften_, the Twenty-eighth Evening (Hersholt numbering) of _Billedbog uden Billeder_, catalogued as SDU-054 (register vid 54) in the register of the H.C. Andersen Centret at Syddansk Universitet, the authoritative source for the Danish texts, titles, and publication history.

## Restrictions

What the production refuses to model, and to whom it delegates.

- **The register is external**: this warrant answers for one staged tale; the full Andersen catalogue and its SDU-054 numbering live in the SDU register, not here.
- **Cross-play context**: strict local isolation. Every link stays within this play's directory.
