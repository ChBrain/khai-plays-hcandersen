# Twenty-Ninth Evening: Reference

## Line of Work

Staging Hans Christian Andersen's _Niogtyvende Aften_ (SDU-055). Månen lyser ned i Vreta klosterkirkes hvælving, hvor kongerne sover under en forgyldt trækrone, og en fremmed spørger om deres navne.

## Origin

Hans Christian Andersen, _Niogtyvende Aften_, the Twenty-Ninth Evening (Hersholt numbering) of _Billedbog uden Billeder_, catalogued as SDU-055 (register vid 55) in the register of the H.C. Andersen Centret at Syddansk Universitet, the authoritative source for the Danish texts, titles, and publication history.

## Restrictions

What the production refuses to model, and to whom it delegates.

- **The register is external**: this warrant answers for one staged tale; the full Andersen catalogue and its SDU-055 numbering live in the SDU register, not here.
- **Cross-play context**: strict local isolation. Every link stays within this play's directory.
