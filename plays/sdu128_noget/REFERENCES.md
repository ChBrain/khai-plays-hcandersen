# Something: Reference

## Line of Work

Staging Hans Christian Andersen's _Noget_ (SDU-128). Fem brødre søger at blive noget i verden, men ved Himmelporten vejer en simpel mursten tungere end de andres store ord.

## Origin

Hans Christian Andersen, _Noget_, catalogued as SDU-128 in the register of the H.C. Andersen Centret at Syddansk Universitet, the authoritative source for the Danish texts, titles, and publication history.

## Restrictions

What the production refuses to model, and to whom it delegates.

- **The register is external**: this warrant answers for one staged tale; the full Andersen catalogue and its SDU-128 numbering live in the SDU register, not here.
- **Cross-play context**: strict local isolation. Every link stays within this play's directory.
