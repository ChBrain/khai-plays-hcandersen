# The Wind Tells of Valdemar Daae and His Daughters: Reference

## Line of Work

Staging Hans Christian Andersen's _Vinden fortæller om Valdemar Daae og hans Døtre_ (SDU-134). En beretning om herremanden Valdemar Daae, der forarmes af sin besættelse af alkymien, indtil vinden blæser alt bort.

## Origin

Hans Christian Andersen, _Vinden fortæller om Valdemar Daae og hans Døtre_, catalogued as SDU-134 in the register of the H.C. Andersen Centret at Syddansk Universitet, the authoritative source for the Danish texts, titles, and publication history.

## Restrictions

What the production refuses to model, and to whom it delegates.

- **The register is external**: this warrant answers for one staged tale; the full Andersen catalogue and its SDU-134 numbering live in the SDU register, not here.
- **Cross-play context**: strict local isolation. Every link stays within this play's directory.
