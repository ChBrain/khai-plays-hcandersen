# Thirty-Third Evening: Reference

## Line of Work

Staging Hans Christian Andersen's _Tre og tredivte Aften_ (SDU-087). Månen ser en fireårig pige bede sin aftenbøn og indrømme, hun ønsker rigeligt smør på sit daglige brød.

## Origin

Hans Christian Andersen, _Tre og tredivte Aften_, the Thirty-third Evening (Hersholt numbering) of _Billedbog uden Billeder_, catalogued as SDU-087 (register vid 87) in the register of the H.C. Andersen Centret at Syddansk Universitet, the authoritative source for the Danish texts, titles, and publication history.

## Restrictions

What the production refuses to model, and to whom it delegates.

- **The register is external**: this warrant answers for one staged tale; the full Andersen catalogue and its SDU-087 numbering live in the SDU register, not here.
- **Cross-play context**: strict local isolation. Every link stays within this play's directory.
