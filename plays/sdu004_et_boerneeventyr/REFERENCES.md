# Et Børneeventyr: Reference

## Line of Work

Staging Hans Christian Andersen's _Et Børneeventyr_ (SDU-004), the fourth chapter of _Skyggebilleder_ (1831). En godtroende konge lover sin datter til den, der lyver ham en løgn, og kun et tysk sørgespil, genfortalt af en rejsende fortæller, kan omsider løse ham.

## Origin

Hans Christian Andersen, _Et Børneeventyr_, the fourth chapter of _Skyggebilleder af en Reise til Harzen_ (published 19 September 1831), catalogued as SDU-004 (register vid 4) in the register of the H.C. Andersen Centret at Syddansk Universitet, the authoritative source for the Danish texts, titles, and publication history. The full text is read at the Centre's own edition page and verified against it.

## Restrictions

What the production refuses to model, and to whom it delegates.

- **The register is external**: this warrant answers for one staged tale; the full Andersen catalogue and its SDU-004 numbering live in the SDU register, not here.
- **Cross-play context**: strict local isolation. Every link stays within this play's directory.
