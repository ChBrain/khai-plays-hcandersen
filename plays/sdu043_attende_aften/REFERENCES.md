# Eighteenth Evening: Reference

## Line of Work

Staging Hans Christian Andersen's _Attende Aften_ (SDU-043). Månen viser Venedig, havets spøgelsesby, hvor tåge, tomme sale og en bunden løve minder om en druknet konges død.

## Origin

Hans Christian Andersen, _Attende Aften_, the Eighteenth Evening (Hersholt numbering) of _Billedbog uden Billeder_, catalogued as SDU-043 (register vid 43) in the register of the H.C. Andersen Centret at Syddansk Universitet, the authoritative source for the Danish texts, titles, and publication history.

## Restrictions

What the production refuses to model, and to whom it delegates.

- **The register is external**: this warrant answers for one staged tale; the full Andersen catalogue and its SDU-043 numbering live in the SDU register, not here.
- **Cross-play context**: strict local isolation. Every link stays within this play's directory.
