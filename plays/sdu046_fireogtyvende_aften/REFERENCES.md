# Twenty-Fourth Evening: Reference

## Line of Work

Staging Hans Christian Andersen's _Fireogtyvende Aften_ (SDU-046). Månen ser den lille Bertel Thorvaldsen spinde ved moderens rok, og genkender ham siden som den hyldede billedhugger.

## Origin

Hans Christian Andersen, _Fireogtyvende Aften_, the Twenty-fourth Evening (Hersholt numbering) of _Billedbog uden Billeder_, catalogued as SDU-046 (register vid 46) in the register of the H.C. Andersen Centret at Syddansk Universitet, the authoritative source for the Danish texts, titles, and publication history.

## Restrictions

What the production refuses to model, and to whom it delegates.

- **The register is external**: this warrant answers for one staged tale, including its one real historical persona; the full Andersen catalogue, its SDU-046 numbering, and Thorvaldsen's biography live outside this board.
- **Cross-play context**: strict local isolation. Every link stays within this play's directory.
